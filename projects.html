PC Build

For this project, I did everything from researching the parts to use to the installation and setup. Below is the complete build list:

  • G.Skill Ripjaws V 16GB (2 x 8GB) DDR4 RAM
  • Cooler Master Hyper 212 RGB CPU Cooler
  • Intel Core i9-11900K Processor
  • Cooler Master Halo Duo-Ring aRGB Case Fan x2

CMMC Certification

Currently I am an intern at an accounting firm on their IT Governance team. My team recently led the effort for the firm to achieve the Level Two CMMC Certification, a certification created by the DoD to ensure that institutions have the appropriate safeguards to work with data that may be important to national security interests. For my role on the project, myself and one other person assembled audit information for over 1200 applications across over 100 controls. Since the audit, I have been working to catalog and maintain changes to the security policy, the implementation of applications, and the product managers.
